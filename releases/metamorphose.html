Metamorphose

Metamorphose brings together previously unreleased music created between 2012 and 2022. The album is built on contrasts: dynamic, cinematic breakcore gives way to dreamlike ambient, while harsh dystopian hip-hop later dissolves into meditative synthwave. Written across different periods of the artist’s life, these tracks reflect solitude, memory and the attempt to understand the person left behind. Yet even in its darkest moments, the album carries a quiet sense of hope – the possibility that change does not only mean losing something, but becoming something new.
